ME?

Chaos Mi is a concept artist and visual developer specializing in entertainment and game industry. His work focuses on environment design, character design, and IP development.

With a background in both digital art and traditional painting, he creates immersive visual worlds that blend artistic vision with technical precision. His art explores the connection between human emotions and visual storytelling, striving to create works that resonate with viewers on a deeper level. Each piece is crafted with the belief that art should not only inspire imagination but also forge emotional connections.
